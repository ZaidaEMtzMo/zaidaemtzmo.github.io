Since I was very young, I have liked animals and live itself. As I grew up, I wanted to be different things: a doctor, a veterinarian, a marine biologist, a Nobel prize winner for finding the cure of cancer... Finally, when I had to decide on a Bachelor's degree, I chose Biology. In the last few years of my undergraduate studies, I specialized in Cellular Biology and wrote my thesis on the early activation of autophagy in response to DNA damage caused by Irinotecan in mammal cells.
